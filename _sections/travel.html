Planes

We recommend flying to PHL. A taxi to center city is a flat rate of $28.50.

Trains

Amtrak will take you to 30th St. Station which is a short walk taxi ride to Water Works or The Logan Hotel. Alternatively, you can take Regional Rail to Suburban Station which is about a five minute walk to the hotel.
